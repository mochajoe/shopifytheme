1. Go to **Theme Customizer** (click **Customize** button)

## üí° Pro Tips

### Product Selection Strategy:
- **Mix price points** - High ($500+), Medium ($100-$300), Low (under $100)
- **Show variety** - Engine, suspension, exterior, etc.
- **Feature bestsellers** - Your most popular items
- **Highlight new arrivals** - Keep it fresh
- **Seasonal rotation** - Update quarterly

### Image Quality:
- Make sure product images are **high-quality**
- **Square images** work best (1:1 aspect ratio)
- **White or transparent backgrounds** look professional
- **At least 800x800px** for sharp display

### Product Organization:
- **Tag products** in Shopify (featured, bestseller, new)
- Create **collections** by vehicle or category
- Update featured products **monthly**
- Track which products get clicks
